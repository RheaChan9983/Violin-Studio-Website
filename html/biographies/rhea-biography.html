Rhea's Biography

About Me

Rhea is currently a third year student at CSU studying Music and Computer Science. At the end of Spring 2021, she will have completed the String Pedagogy Program at CSU under the instruction of Ms. Leslie Stewart. Her expected graduation is May 2023.

Rhea started violin at the age of 9, after following the steps of her older cousins who also played the violin. She immediately fell in love with the violin. She learned the violin through her elementary school orchestra and contiuned to learn the violin through her public school orchestras.

She was concertmaster of the Chaparral High School Orchestra her senior year of high school. She has also played with the Continental League Honor Orchestra and was Principle Second Violinist for the 2017-1018 Continental League Honor Orchestra. In Spring 2018, she was admitted into the Colorado State University Violin Program.

She currently studies violin under the instruction of Dr. Ron Francois at Colorado State University. She also currently plays with the Colorado State University Symphony Orchestra. She is part of the String Pedagogy Program under the instruction of Professor Leslie Stewart. She is a double major in Computer Science. She will graduate Colorado State University in May 2023 with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts in Music.

To Rhea, teaching music is a way to share her love and passion of music to those who choose to learn the violin. Music has been an amazing part of her life and has given her so many opportunities to meet some amazing people and friends and has open so many doors. She hopes to pass this on and share this with her students.

In the future Rhea would love to have a violin studio in Denver and she would love to play in a pit orchestra for musical productions!
