Nancy's Biography

About Me

Originally from Mexico, Nancy started her music studies at the age of 13 in Orizaba, her hometown city, with Professor Diana Fonseca.In 2013 she obtained her Bachelor of Arts in Music degree at the University of Veracruz under the guidance of Prof. Mikhail Medvid.

Nancy holds more than 12 years of experience as an orchestral and chamber music musician, before coming to the United States she was part of the violin section of many professional orchestras in Mexico, such as Xalapa Symphony Orchestra, Boca del RÃ­o Philharmonic Orchestra, she also dedicated part of her music education to travel overseas as an active performer, she also was part of many other international orchestras and ensembles such as the JOA (Jeune Orchestre Atlantique) with specialty on interpretation of classical and romantic repertoire in France, an active performer at the Euro Music Festival & Academy Halle (Saale) in Germany, and with the Xalapa Symphony Orchestra as part of its international tour in Brazil, performing in the most important music halls of the country. \

Nancy started teaching over her years of college, she truly believes in the limitless possibilities that students have to strive for excellence by sharing music and inspiring them based on joy, acceptance, inclusion, and commitment, within a positive and nurturing environment.

In 2020, she graduated with her Masters in Music Performance degree at Colorado State University under the instruction of Ron Francois in violin and under the instruction of Prof. Leslie Stuart in violin pedagogy. Nancy is currently a Teaching Artist at El Sistema Colorado in Denver, and a Violin Teacher at Crescendo Fine Arts Academy, as well as a violinist of Longmont Symphony Orchestra in the city of Longmont.
