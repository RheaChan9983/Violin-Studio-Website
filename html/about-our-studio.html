About Our Studio

Lessons That We Teach

Everyone needs something a little different, from learning how to hold a violin to making your way through Bach's Sonatas and Partitas.

Beginner

Beginning lessons consist of learning how to hold the violin, plucking and bowing and learning the basics of reading music. By the time the student is done with the beginning phase, the student will be able to be proficient at reading music and being able to pluck and bow on their violin

Intermediate

Intermediate lessons will be where we start to introduce more technical materials and will have more of a focus on etudes, scales and arpeggios, vibrato, and starting to look at solo violin material. Music theory will also be more incorporated into the curriculum to give the student a better understanding of the music they will be learning.
